Damped Oscillation
Like simple harmonic motion but amplitude decreases over time, simulating friction or resistance.

Driven (Forced) Oscillation
Oscillation influenced by an external periodic force, possibly causing resonance.

Sawtooth or Triangle Wave Motion
The circle moves linearly from one edge to the other and snaps back (sawtooth) or reverses direction linearly (triangle).

Random Step Oscillation
The circle jumps to random points along the axis, pausing between jumps.

Nonlinear Oscillation
E.g., motion governed by sine of sine or other nonlinear functions for more complex patterns.

Lissajous Curve Oscillation
Moving along two perpendicular simple harmonic motions at different frequencies to create a looping pattern.

Exponential Oscillation
Motion where displacement grows or shrinks exponentially with time.

Chaotic Oscillation
Using a chaotic function (like logistic map values) to determine position — unpredictable yet deterministic.
